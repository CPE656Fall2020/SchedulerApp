Interface IByteStreamProcessor

IByteStreamProcessor defines a generic device that performs a specific operation of a stream of data. Byte Processing Devices have a defined throughput rate and known energy consumption rate, varying according to the length of the stream being processed. These profiles are uniquely identifiable across the entire scheduling system.
